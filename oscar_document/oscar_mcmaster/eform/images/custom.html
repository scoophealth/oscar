This is a sample template that you can customise to your purposes. Simply
  1. Open the custom.html file (found in your eForm images) in a html editor such as... this one found in Oscar! (vi or KompoZer are other html editors) and change whatever you want between the <body> tags.</body>
  2. Yeah you can use style elements, practically anything you like, it will be supported in Letter, only the simple stuff is exampled here.
  3. Use any of the OSCAR fields by enclosing them with pairs of  ## signs and they will auto populate from the database. (sample database fields and their values for the patient in whose chart you opened this template are listed below)
  4. Make your own fields by enclosing your prompt with pairs of ## signs and the user will be prompted for the value
  5. Save your version of custom.html to the hard drive.
  6. Delete the custom.html in your eForms images and upload your version of custom.html and you are off!
  Enjoy! (PS you can edit any of the templates this way)
